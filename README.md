## 🧠 About Me

I'm **Shankar** — an AI/ML engineer, cloud architect, and full-stack developer working at the intersection of **Artificial Intelligence**, **Cloud Computing**, and **Web Development**.

My work spans industries like automotive, healthcare, insurance, and industrial automation — building things like real-time AI diagnostics for connected vehicles, virtual health assistants, AI-driven drug discovery pipelines, and privacy-preserving ML on electronic health records.

I'm also an **inventor** — my patented system **CAKE (Change Assessment Knowledge Engine)** is actively used by scientists in pharmaceutical manufacturing. Across my career, I've published peer-reviewed research, authored multiple books, and delivered enterprise-grade solutions using **React**, **Node.js**, **AWS**, **Azure**, and **GCP**.

> 💡 *Simply put: I build intelligent systems that solve real problems — and I do it with both engineering rigor and a user-first mindset.*

### ⚡ Fun Facts About Me

- 🚀 **14+ years** of experience building enterprise-grade solutions across 4+ industries
- 📚 Published researcher, book author, and patent holder (CAKE system used in pharma manufacturing)
- 🌐 Active content creator — sharing insights on [Medium](https://medium.com/@raviishankargarapti), [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=oh4-uKYAAAAJ&hl=en), and [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ravigarapati)
